A Few Words About Me

I am a Sophomore Creative Technologies and Buisness Administration Major with a Minor in general Music. Through the years, Music has always been the most influencial apsect of my life. This is where I aquired my friends, my life skills and devoted most of my time. In this time, I also built up a fairly solid ensemble track record. As a student of Mendota Township Highschool, I was apart of every group that I could be: Concert Band, Marching Band, Jazz Band, Percussion Choir, Concert Choir, Madrigals, Musicals, and Showchoir. In my career, I hope to find a job that revolves around Sound Design so that I can keep this love for music in my everyday life. I am also interesting in building a skill set around the entirety of the CTK Major. Whether its web design, game design or any other of the numerous options, it is too soon to say.
